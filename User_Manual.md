# LDM College ERP - User Manual & SOPs

Welcome to the **LDM College Management System**. This document provides a non-technical guide to managing your college's digital operations, from finance to student academic tracking.

---

## 1. System Overview & Access

The ERP is designed with four primary user roles:
- **Administrator**: Full control over finance, users, and campus settings.
- **Teacher/Faculty**: Manage student attendance and academic records.
- **Student**: View fee status, attendance, and college updates.
- **Employee**: Access to specific administrative functions as assigned.

### Login Details
- **URL**: [ldmcollege.com](https://ldmcollege.com)
- **Admin Credentials**: Provided during handover.
- **Teacher/Student Credentials**: Generated by the Administrator.

## 2. Administrator Guide (Standard Operating Procedures)

### ğŸ’° Finance Management
The finance module tracks the college's revenue and expenses.

#### **Step 1: Setting up Fee Structures**
Before students can pay, you must define the fees for each program.
1. Navigate to **Finance > Fee Structures**.
2. Click **Add New Fee Structure**.
3. Select the **Program** (e.g., DMLT), **Session** (2024-2025), and **Semester**.
4. Set the **Total Amount** and **Due Date**.
5. Save the structure.

#### **Step 2: Recording Student Payments**
When a student pays their fees:
1. Go to **Finance > Payments**.
2. Find the student and click **Manage**.
3. Record the amount paid, payment mode (Cash/Online), and transaction date.
4. The system will automatically update the student's **Pending Balance**.

#### **Step 3: Managing Expenses**
Track every rupee spent on college operations.
1. Go to **Finance > Expenses**.
2. Click **Record New Expense**.
3. Enter the **Title**, **Amount**, **Category** (e.g., Utilities, Maintenance), and **Recipient**.
4. These entries will automatically appear in your **Finance Dashboard** summary.

#### **Step 4: Disbursing Salaries**
1. Go to **Finance > Salaries**.
2. Select the **Month** and **Employee**.
3. Record the **Base Amount** and any **Deductions**.
4. Mark as **Paid** once the transfer is complete.

## 4. Best Practices for Data Entry

1. **Be Consistent**: Use clear titles for expenses (e.g., "Electricity Bill Jan 2025" vs "Bill").
2. **Review Dashboard Daily**: Check the **Net Balance** on the Finance Dashboard to monitor college health.
3. **Keep it Dynamic**: Regularly update the **Gallery** and **Marquee Updates** on the homepage to engage new admissions.
